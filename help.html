Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I book a car?

You can easily book a car through our website or mobile app in a few simple steps. Just select your dates, location, and choose your preferred vehicle. You'll receive instant confirmation!

What documents are required to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, national ID (CNIC/Passport for Pakistani citizens), and a credit/debit card for payment. For international clients, an International Driving Permit (IDP) might also be required.

What should I do if the car breaks down or there's an accident?

In case of an emergency, immediately call our 24/7 roadside assistance helpline. Our team will reach you promptly to provide the necessary support and guidance.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period, subject to vehicle availability and additional charges. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible to arrange the extension.

Can I rent a car with a professional driver?

Absolutely! You have the option to book a professional driver service during your booking process. Additional charges will apply for this service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), debit cards, and secure online payment gateways. Specific payment options may vary by region.

Is there an age requirement for renting a car?

Yes, the minimum age to rent a car is typically 21 years old. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Please check our terms and conditions for details.

What is your fuel policy?

Our standard fuel policy is 'Full to Full'. You will receive the vehicle with a full tank and are expected to return it with a full tank. Otherwise, refueling charges will apply.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours prior to pickup are typically free of charge. Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows may incur a fee. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms.
